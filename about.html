About Wingman

Wingman Bahrain is a local brand dedicated to serving bold, flavour-packed chicken wings. We combine quality ingredients with creative sauces to deliver an unforgettable experience.


Our mission is to be the go-to place for wings in Bahrain by offering fast online ordering, friendly service and a fun, casual atmosphere.


Our Commitment to Quality every order is prepared with meticulous care, starting from the selection of high-quality chicken down to the precise cooking process. Our goal is not just to serve a meal, but to deliver an unforgettable experience with every visit or delivery order. We are proud to serve our customers in Bahrain and always strive to deliver our best.

Why Customers Love Us

  • Freshly cooked wings made to order.
  • Multiple flavours and heat levels.
  • Easy online ordering for pickup or delivery.
